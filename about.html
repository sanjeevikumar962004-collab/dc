OUR CHRONOLOGY

From a boutique framing studio in San Francisco to state-of-the-art skyscraper developments across Western fault-lines, tracking how we set standards.

2008
FOUNDATION ERA

2008: Structuring Boutique Scaffolding

Established as a small framing and metal structural studio in California. Under Marcus Finch's strict supervision, the team focused on residential framing, setting high benchmarks of raw material selection and OSHA safety compliance.

SCALING ERA

2015: Commercial BIM Transitions

Incorporated Virtual BIM modeling software natively into planning workflows. The studio pivoted from framing to managing complex high-rise structural builds, securing zero-safety hazard compliance audits on all major concrete castings.

SUSTAINABLE ERA

2026: Eco-Evolution & Decarbonization

Fully transitioning our material supply chain to carbon-offset composites, green steel frames, and thermal-efficient insulation properties to pioneer carbon-neutral structural systems.
